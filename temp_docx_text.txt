1. Executive Summary

Abbott's Established Pharmaceuticals Division (EPD) operates across 70+ countries with hundreds of sales representatives, multiple promo lines, and complex quarter-over-quarter incentive structures. Today, incentive calculations are managed through disconnected Excel models and manual approval chains — a process that is slow, error-prone, and impossible to audit at scale.

We propose to design, build, and deploy an enterprise-grade Sales Incentive Compensation (SIC) platform — purpose-built for Abbott EPD's pharmaceutical environment — that will deliver:

Automate incentive calculations in real-time across all countries, promo lines, and products

Enforce a multi-level digital approval workflow (NSM → GM → HR → Finance Director)

Support country-by-country flexible compensation configurations

Scale to 70+ countries through a phased rollout with a proven 4-country POC

Deliver the intelligence of enterprise tools like Salesforce Spiff and Xactly Incent — at a fraction of the cost, without vendor lock-in, and tailored entirely to Abbott's model



  ℹ  The platform will serve as the single source of truth for all incentive data, eliminating manual reconciliation and cutting the monthly close cycle from 2–3 weeks to under 48 hours.

Key Differentiators

1. Flexible Compensation Configuration Engine

Every country can have unique, independently configured:

Incentive formula parameters (Sales %, TCFA %, Field Work %)

Product portfolio weights per Promo Line

Reimbursable % rules by rep category and seniority

Currency handling (LC ↔ USD conversion with live rates)

Maternity/leave proration rules

Quarter weighting (equal or stepped across Q1–Q4)

All configurable via a no-code admin panel — no developer involvement needed for each new country.



2. Multi-Level Digital Approval Workflow

The production platform will implement a fully digital, tracked approval chain:



Rep Data Submitted  →  FLM Review  →  Regional Manager / NSM  →  General Manager  →  HR Manager  →  Finance Director  →  LOCKED & PAID



Features of the approval engine:

Email notifications triggered at each stage transition

Comments and rejection with mandatory reason field

Full audit trail with timestamps and user attribution

Delegation of authority during leave (auto-rerouting)

Conditional routing for above-threshold payouts

Bulk approval for standard low-risk records

Mobile-friendly approval interface



3. Multi-Tenant Global Architecture

The system is built from Day 1 as a multi-tenant, multi-country platform — ready to onboard new countries without any code changes. Country configurations are stored as data, not code.
